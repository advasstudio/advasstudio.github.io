Overview

This privacy policy applies to all apps developed and published by Advas on:

Google Play

I take your privacy seriously. This document explains what data is collected when you use my apps, why it's collected, and how it's handled.

I don't sell your data. I don't share it with anyone except the third-party services listed below, which power ads and analytics in my apps.

Data Collected

I don't directly collect or store any personal information. My apps use the following third-party services which may collect data:

  • Google AdMob — used to serve ads. AdMob may collect your device's advertising ID and usage data to deliver relevant advertisements.
    Google Privacy Policy →
  • Firebase Analytics (Google) — used to understand how people use the app (screens visited, session length, crash reports). Data is anonymous and aggregated.
    Google Privacy Policy →
  • Google Play Services — core platform services that may collect diagnostic and performance data.

I do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or precise location.

How Data Is Used

Data collected through these services is used only to:

  • Show ads that help keep the app free
  • Understand how the app is used so I can improve it
  • Diagnose crashes and fix performance issues

Children's Privacy

My apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through one of my apps, please contact me and I will take steps to address it.

Data Retention

Since I don't directly collect or store personal data on my own servers, there is nothing for me to retain or delete. For data held by Google, please refer to their privacy policy and your Google account settings.

Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights regarding your personal data — including access, correction, or deletion. For data processed by Google, these requests should be directed to them. For anything else, contact me directly.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top. Continued use of my apps after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
